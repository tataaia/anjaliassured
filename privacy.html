Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 08, 2026

At Anjali Assured (led by Anjali Devi, authorized Tata AIA Life Insurance Advisor), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our query form.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us using the consultation/query form on our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your phone number / mobile number
  • Your email address
  • Your selected areas of interest (e.g. term plans, savings schemes)
  • Any message or details you provide regarding your insurance needs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To contact you back to provide the requested insurance consultations.
  • To evaluate which Tata AIA Life Insurance policies best fit your age group, budget, and protection goals.
  • To send you updates regarding your queries.

Note: We do NOT sell, trade, or share your contact details with external third-party marketing companies. Any official submission of insurance proposals is processed directly through Tata AIA Life Insurance official portals with your consent.

3. Information Security

We implement appropriate physical and electronic measures to secure the data we collect online, preventing unauthorized access or disclosure. Client confidentiality is our top priority.

4. Cookies

Our website is a clean static website and does not run trackers or load third-party advertising cookies.
